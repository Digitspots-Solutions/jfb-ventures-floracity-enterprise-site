About Us

JFB Ventures & Floracity Enterprise is a leading manufacturing company based in Oshodi/Isolo, Lagos. We combine years of industry expertise with modern manufacturing processes to deliver exceptional products and services to our clients. Our commitment to quality, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred partner for businesses across Nigeria. Operating from our facility at 12b Biodun Komolafe Street off Fashuen Road by Century Bus Stop, Ago Palace Way, Okota-Isolo, we maintain the highest standards in every project we undertake.
